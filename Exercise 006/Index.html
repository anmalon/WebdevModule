Transport

Trains

Trains are one of the oldest and most efficient ways to transport things. They can be quite inflexible and are quite capital intensive to setup, but after that they are incredibly energy efficient and both reliable and speedy for transport at the cost of flexibility.

    Advantages
  • Energy efficient and reliable transport
  • High space utilisation
    Disadvantages
  • Capital intensive upfront
  • Very inflexible

Planes

Planes are a very capital intensive but flexible form of travel that is used by many companies both for personnel and high value goods transport. They are quite costly but are incredibly safe and almost impossible to beat for speed over longer or even medium distances.

    Advantages
  • Fast transport to many locations
  • High security and safety
    Disadvantages
  • Can be costly
  • Only able to take light or high cost/weight goods

Cars

Cars are one of the most common forms of transport in the modern age, with an estimated 1.4 billion on the road in 2022. They are a relatively inexpensive form of transport and do not require much investment to get started.

    Advantages
  • Very flexible form of transport for short and medium distances
  • Allows for freedom and self-reliance
    Disadvantages
  • Very space inefficient
  • Major cause of pollution
